About Me

I'm Shrawan Thakur, a Bachelor of Science in Computer Science graduate (2026) from Sharda University, Greater Noida, India, with a passion for frontend development and creating modern, responsive, and user-friendly web applications.

I have built a strong foundation in HTML5, CSS3, JavaScript, Bootstrap, Tailwind CSS, Sass, Git, and GitHub, and I'm currently expanding my frontend expertise by learning React.js through hands-on projects and continuous practice. I also have experience with Adobe Illustrator and Adobe Photoshop, which helps me create clean and visually appealing user interfaces.

Each project has strengthened my understanding of responsive design, problem-solving, and writing clean, maintainable code.

As a motivated fresher, I'm seeking an internship or entry-level Frontend Developer opportunity where I can contribute to real-world projects, collaborate with experienced developers, and continue growing my technical skills. My long-term goal is to become a Frontend Developer and eventually grow into an Integration Engineer within the software industry.
